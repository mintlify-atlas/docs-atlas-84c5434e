Get up and running with Minimal in just a few steps. This guide will walk you through creating an account, saving your first bookmark, and organizing with groups.

## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up at minimal.so">
    Visit [minimal.so](https://minimal.so) and create your account. You can sign up with:
    
    - **Email and password**: Enter your email and choose a password
    - **Google OAuth**: Sign in with your Google account for faster setup
    
    <Note>
      No credit card required. Your bookmarks are private by default.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    If you signed up with email, check your inbox for a verification email. Click the link to verify your account.
    
    <Tip>
      Didn't receive the email? Check your spam folder or request a new verification email from the login page.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
    Once logged in, you'll see your dashboard with an input field to save bookmarks. Your account comes with a default "Bookmarks" group.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Save your first bookmark

<Steps>
  <Step title="Paste a URL">
    In the input field at the top of your dashboard, paste any URL you want to save. For example:
    
    ```
    https://github.com/ephraimduncan/minimal.so
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Press Enter">
    Hit **Enter** or **Cmd/Ctrl+Enter** to save. Minimal automatically:
    
    - Fetches the page title
    - Extracts the description
    - Downloads the favicon
    - Normalizes the URL for deduplication
    
    <Note>
      If the URL already exists in your bookmarks, it will be updated instead of creating a duplicate.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View your bookmark">
    Your bookmark appears in the list below. You can:
    
    - Click to open the URL in a new tab
    - Right-click (or hold) for options: Copy, Rename, Move, Delete
    - Press **Space** to select multiple bookmarks for bulk operations
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Organize with groups

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new group">
    Click the **+** button next to "Groups" in the sidebar to create a new group. Give it a name like "Work", "Reading List", or "Design Resources".
    
    <Tip>
      Groups are automatically assigned a color from a predefined palette. You can change the color later in group settings.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Move bookmarks to groups">
    To move a bookmark to a different group:
    
    1. Right-click the bookmark (or press **Space** to select multiple)
    2. Select **Move to group**
    3. Choose the destination group
    
    <Note>
      When you move a bookmark to a different group, its visibility resets to the group's default (typically private).
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Switch between groups">
    Click any group in the sidebar to view its bookmarks. The URL updates to reflect the current group, so you can bookmark and share specific group views.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Learn keyboard shortcuts

Minimal is keyboard-first. Here are the essential shortcuts:

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **↑ / ↓** | Navigate bookmarks |
| **Enter** | Open bookmark |
| **Cmd/Ctrl+C** | Copy URL |
| **Cmd/Ctrl+E** | Rename bookmark |
| **Cmd/Ctrl+⌫** | Delete bookmark |
| **Space** | Select for bulk actions |
| **Cmd/Ctrl+A** | Select all bookmarks |
| **Escape** | Deselect all |

<Tip>
  See the full list of keyboard shortcuts in [Keyboard Shortcuts](/features/keyboard-shortcuts).
</Tip>
